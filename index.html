Frequently asked questions

The short version.

What does Air Note do?

Air Note captures meetings, voice notes, ideas and messages in one place. It organizes those details into useful context you can bring into Claude.

How do I capture a meeting without a bot?

Open Air Note and tap record while the conversation is happening. Air Note creates clear notes, decisions and next steps without adding another attendee to the call.

Can Claude answer questions about earlier meetings?

Yes. Once Air Note is connected, Claude can use your saved meetings, messages and ideas as context when answering questions, writing plans or helping you follow up.

What can I send through WhatsApp?

You can message a thought, send a voice note or forward something important. Air Note captures it so the information is ready when you need it later.

When is Air Note available?

Air Note is currently inviting early users, with iPhone access first. Join the early access list to hear when your place is ready.
